1. Information We Collect

We may process only minimal user data, limited to what is necessary to provide the Mobile Application and Services. This includes:

Location Data:

  • Approximate Location: We collect this data to show nearby historical sites and provide location-based services.
  • Precise Location: We collect this data to provide detailed site recommendations and enable features such as Discover Mode and Plan Journey.

Location data is shared only with third-party services (e.g., Google Maps) to provide navigation and similar functionality.

Camera Access:

We request access to your device’s camera only with your explicit consent. This is used for augmented reality features (e.g., Try Origin AR).

Photos and Media:

The app includes a history diary feature where users can save photos locally. These photos are not collected, shared, or transmitted by us.

We do not collect any personal information unless necessary for core functionalities of the App, and we never share or sell user data.

2. How We Use Collected Information

We use the collected data solely for functionality and improving the App. Specifically:

  • App Functionality: To show nearby historical sites, provide site information, and allow users to plan journeys.
  • Discover Mode: To notify users of nearby historical sites based on location, including background location access (if permission is granted).
  • Plan Journey: To send the user's location to Google Maps for directions.

No personal data or media are accessed beyond what is required for these functionalities.

3. Permissions We Use and Why

  • INTERNET: Used to retrieve images of historical sites from an Amazon S3 server and access Google Maps for the Plan Journey feature.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION & ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: Required to provide location-based features such as Discover Mode and Plan Journey.
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION: Used for Discover Mode, allowing the app to notify users of nearby sites when running in the background.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Sends notifications when users are near historical sites (with explicit permission).
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: Supports Discover Mode by allowing background services to run.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE & WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: Allows users to save photos to their device (locally) for the History Diary.
  • CAMERA: Required for the Try Origin AR feature, allowing users to view AR content.
